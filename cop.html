Customer Onboarding Process

EzyHelpers - Streamlined Customer Onboarding

1
Lead Entry
  • Add lead to CRM/Customer Tracker
  • Record name and contact information
  • Note source of lead
2
Initial Contact
  • Call customer to introduce EzyHelpers
  • Explain our services and process
3
Requirements Gathering
  • Type of help needed (Maid/Nanny/etc.)
  • Working hours and days
  • Gender and age preference
  • Specific work requirements
  • Salary range expectations
  • Preferred location
4
Pricing & Terms
  • ₹1999 Registration Fee (non-refundable)
  • Service Fee details (upon placement)
  • Replacement policy explanation
5
Payment Collection
  • Send payment link to customer
  • Wait for payment confirmation
  • Update status to 'Registered – Active'
6
Profile Matching
  • Share 2-3 suitable helper profiles
  • Include photos and experience details
  • Provide location information
7
Conference Call
  • Arrange call with customer and helper
  • Include sales executive for support
  • Ensure mutual comfort and understanding
8
Customer Decision
  • Confirm customer's satisfaction with helper
  • If not satisfied, return to profile matching
9
Placement Confirmation
  • Update CRM status to 'Confirmed & Placed'
  • Collect final Service Fee
10
Contact Handover
  • Share contact information between parties
  • Set clear expectations for both sides
11
Follow-up
  • Conduct 1-week check-in
  • Address any concerns or issues
  • Ensure smooth working relationship
